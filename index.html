Transposing Guide

Concert Pitch to Alto Saxophone

The Alto Saxophone is an Eb instrument. This means that a written C played on the saxophone sounds exactly like an Eb played on a piano. To transpose from Concert Pitch (Piano, Flute, Violin, Guitar) to Alto Sax, move DOWN a minor 3rd (and drop an octave) or move UP a major 6th.
